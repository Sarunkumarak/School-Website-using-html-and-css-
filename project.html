About Little Genius

At Little Genius Play School, we believe in nurturing young minds through creativity, compassion, and curiosity. Our warm, safe, and joyful environment is designed to encourage natural exploration and holistic development in children aged 2 to 6.

Inspired by the purity and strength of the Himalayas, our philosophy combines nature-based learning, hands-on activities, and structured play to build strong emotional, intellectual, and social foundations.

From storytelling and sensory play to early science and literacy, every experience at Little Genius is crafted to help children discover themselves and the world around them with confidence and joy.

Our dedicated team of trained educators ensures personalized attention, enabling every child to shine in their own unique way.
